# Go-to-Market Strategy: Japan

## Core Strategy: Elegance, Precision, and Trust

The Japanese market values quality, aesthetics, and flawless service (*omotenashi*). Our platform must embody these principles.

## Key Features

1. **Reservation System First:** A sophisticated, real-time reservation management system is more critical than online ordering for many establishments.
   - Manage table inventory, seating times, and course selections.
   - Automated, polite confirmations and reminders in formal Japanese (*keigo*).

2. **Aesthetic & Minimalist Templates:**
   - Designs based on Japanese principles: minimalism, natural materials, beautiful typography.
   - Focus on high-quality photo galleries.

3. **Deep Local Integrations:**
   - **Tabelog & Gurunavi:** Sync menu, hours, and reservation availability.
   - **LINE Official Account:** Allow customers to reserve or get updates directly via LINE.
   - **Payment:** Integrate with PayPay, Merpay, and JCB in addition to standard credit cards.

4. **Features for Precision:**
   - **Seasonal & Daily Menus:** Easy-to-update sections for "Today's Special" or seasonal menus.
   - **Detailed Allergen Information:** Build trust by providing structured, clear ingredient information.
   - **Bilingual Menu Management:** Simple interface to manage both Japanese and English menus for tourist areas.
