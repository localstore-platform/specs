# Go-to-Market Strategy: Vietnam

## Core Strategy: Mobile-First, Simplicity, and Deep Social/Payment Integration

Small business owners in Vietnam run their operations from a smartphone. Our platform must be a simple, fast, and integrated mobile tool.

## Key Features

1. **Zalo & Facebook as Core Features:**
   - **Zalo Mini App:** Generate a Zalo Mini App for ordering and payments within Zalo. This is a killer feature.
   - **Facebook Messenger Bot:** Automatically create a chatbot for taking orders on the store's Facebook page.
   - **Social Login:** Prioritize Zalo/Facebook for signup and login.

2. **Hyper-Local Payment & Delivery Gateways:**
   - **Payments:** Direct integration with **MoMo, ZaloPay, and VNPay** is essential.
   - **Delivery:** Integrate with **GrabFood, ShopeeFood, and Gojek** APIs.

3. **True "Mobile-First" Design:**
   - The admin dashboard must be flawless on a low-cost Android phone over a 4G connection.
   - Templates are designed for vertical viewing.
   - Built-in QR code generation for table-top menus.

4. **Culturally-Aware Templates & Pricing:**
   - Vibrant, street-food-friendly designs.
   - Extremely competitive pricing, including a robust free tier with a pay-per-transaction model.
