# Feature Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP (3-4 Months)

- **Goal:** Launch a functional product for a small group of beta testers in Vietnam.
- **Core Features:**
  - User authentication (Zalo/Email) & Tenant management.
  - Mobile-first Admin Dashboard.
  - Basic website builder with 3-5 Vietnamese-centric templates.
  - Menu management (CRUD for items/categories).
  - Image uploads.
  - Subdomain publishing (`store.ourplatform.com`).
  - Subscription management (basic tiering).

## Phase 2: Japan Launch & Enhanced Features (3-4 Months)

- **Goal:** Launch in Japan and add high-value features for both markets.
- **Features:**
  - **Japan:** Reservation System, Japanese templates, LINE integration.
  - **Vietnam:** Zalo Mini App generation, Facebook Messenger bot.
  - **Both:** Custom domain support, more templates, basic analytics.

## Phase 3: E-commerce & Advanced Integrations (Ongoing)

- **Goal:** Deepen the platform's value and expand revenue streams.
- **Features:**
  - Full online ordering & shopping cart.
  - Payment gateway integration (MoMo, VNPay, PayPay).
  - Delivery service integration (GrabFood, etc.).
  - SEO tools.
  - Customer reviews module.
