# Monetization Strategy

## Guiding Principles

- **Low Barrier to Entry:** A strong free tier is essential to capture the price-sensitive market.
- **Value-Based Upselling:** Tiers are structured around features that directly help businesses make more money.
- **Local Pricing:** Prices will be set and displayed in local currency (VND and JPY).

## Tiered Pricing Model

### Free Tier ("Starter")

- **Target:** New businesses, testing the platform.
- **Features:**
  - 1 website with subdomain.
  - Up to 20 menu items.
  - Basic templates.
  - Platform branding displayed.
- **Monetization:** Optional transaction fee (e.g., 2-3%) on orders placed through the site.

### Basic Tier ("Growth")

- **Price:** ~$10-15 USD/month (localized currency).
- **Target:** Businesses ready to establish a professional online presence.
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Free.
  - Custom domain support.
  - Unlimited menu items.
  - All templates.
  - Remove platform branding.
  - Basic analytics.
  - **Key Upsell:** Reservation System (for Japan), Zalo Mini App (for Vietnam).

### Pro Tier ("Advanced")

- **Price:** ~$30-50 USD/month (localized currency).
- **Target:** Businesses focused on online sales and delivery.
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Growth.
  - Full online ordering & payment gateway integration.
  - Delivery service integration.
  - Advanced analytics & SEO tools.
  - Priority support.
